## üí¨ Response Guidelines

### Keep It SHORT
- **Max 2 sentences** per response
- AI is optimized for quick audio
- Concise = better TTS quality

### Be Professional
- Always acknowledge the issue
- Show empathy: "I understand your concern"
- Offer solutions: "Let me help you with that"

### Ask Clarifying Questions
- Order number?
- Tracking ID?
- When did you order?
- What's the exact error message?

### End Properly
- "Is there anything else I can help you with?"
- "Thank you for calling Lazada"

## üé≠ Practice Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Late Delivery
**Customer**: "My package was supposed to arrive yesterday, but it's still not here."

**Good Response**:
> "I apologize for the delay. Can you provide your tracking number so I can check the status?"

**Bad Response** ‚ùå:
> "Hmm, that's unfortunate. Delays happen sometimes due to traffic, weather, or high volume..."

### Scenario 2: Wrong Item
**Customer**: "I ordered a blue shirt but received a red one."

**Good Response**:
> "I'm sorry about that mistake. Please provide your order number, and I'll arrange a return and replacement."

### Scenario 3: App Error
**Customer**: "The Lazada app won't open on my phone."

**Good Response**:
> "I understand that's frustrating. Have you tried clearing the app cache or reinstalling it?"

### Scenario 4: Off-Topic (Decline)
**Customer**: "How do I become a Lazada seller?"

**Good Response**:
> "I apologize, but I can only assist with delivery, order, or app issues. For seller inquiries, please contact our main hotline at 123-4567."

## üèÜ Scoring Criteria

You'll be evaluated on **4 dimensions** (0-100 each):

### 1. Overall Performance
- Did you solve the customer's problem?
- Was the conversation smooth?
- Professional throughout?

### 2. Empathy Score
- Did you acknowledge feelings?
- Used empathetic language: "I understand", "I apologize"
- Showed genuine care?

### 3. Clarity Score
- Clear, concise responses
- No jargon or confusion
- Easy to understand?

### 4. Problem-Solving Score
- Asked relevant questions
- Gathered necessary info (order #, tracking ID)
- Offered concrete solutions?

## üéì Practice Tips

### Before Each Call
1. Take a deep breath
2. Remember: Delivery, Orders, App ONLY
3. Have notepad ready for order numbers

### During the Call
1. Listen carefully to the issue
2. Identify which category (Delivery/Order/App)
3. Ask ONE clarifying question
4. Provide solution or next steps
5. Confirm customer is satisfied

### After the Call
1. Review your transcript
2. Check your scores
3. Identify areas to improve
4. Practice difficult scenarios again

## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Practice the decline**  
   You'll hear off-topic questions often. Master the polite decline.

2. **Use customer's language**  
   If they say "parcel", you say "parcel". If they say "package", say "package".

3. **Stay calm with angry customers**  
   Empathy first: "I understand this is frustrating..."

   Only commit to what you can deliver.

5. **End on a positive note**  
   Always ask: "Is there anything else I can help you with?"
